Darson and Sons – Gas & Dining

The name reflects the core services of the business in a straightforward manner, combining both the cooking gas refilling and restaurant dining services. It conveys the message of reliability and versatility, which is essential for appealing to a wide range of customers seeking either gas refills or a dining experience.

Site Purpose

  • Provide information about Darson and Sons’ dual services (gas refilling and restaurant dining).
  • Allow customers to easily book a table at the restaurant and schedule a gas refill.
  • Promote special offers and services, such as combo deals and seasonal promotions.
  • Provide customer support through the Contact Us section, offering a direct way to reach out for inquiries or service needs.

Scenario

  • Are customers looking for a reliable and safe place to refill their cooking gas, with transparent pricing and convenient scheduling options?
  • Do you want a cozy place to dine in or enjoy a delicious meal at home with easy online ordering and reservations?

Color Shema

  • Primary Color: Warm Yellow (#FFCC00). This color will be used for call-to-action buttons, hover effects, and highlights. It conveys warmth and energy, ideal for food and customer service-related elements.
  • Secondary Color: Deep Charcoal (#333333). This color will be used for text, headers, and backgrounds to maintain a professional, modern, and clean aesthetic.

Typography

  • Lato (Sans-serif): Bold Lato to make headings stand out and give a clean, modern look.
  • Body Text: Regular Lato for easy readability and a cohesive design.
  • Buttons & Calls to Action: Lato, bold or medium weight for emphasis and legibility.
